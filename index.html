 What is elyps?

We are a mobile app that satisfies your everyday banking needs whilst opening the door to the world of cryptocurrency.


 Why should I switch my banking to elyps?

We are the simplest and most secure on the market, with a promise to keep the customer at the heart of what we do. Always.


 Why is elyps using a blockchain?

elyps has created a private blockchain to enable fast & fully secure transactions between its users. elyps is also connected to public blockchains to enable transactions with non-elyps users worldwide.


 What is a blockchain?

A blockchain is a decentralized database that continuously updates digital records of who owns what. It does not need to have a central administrator like traditional databases. The content is encrypted and cannot be removed. It is therefore considered as the safest way to store data.
Blockchain networks can be private (like elyps) or public (like bitcoin).


 Is it safe to trade cryptocurrencies?

Trading cryptocurrencies is absolutely safe when using a regulated financial institution such as elyps. Unlike unregulated players, elyps has to continuously prove that its technical platform is compliant with the most severe security standards.
